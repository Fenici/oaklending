About us

Oak Lending is a Sydney based Australian finance compan.

“ We provide full and specific solution for every client. ”



Oak Lending is a Sydney based Australian finance company specialising in tailored lending and mortgage solutions for a variety of borrowers.

We have over 60 years of combined experience in the mortgage and insurance industries and strive to obtain the best possible home loan and interest rate solution suited to your investment strategy or lifestyle.

Oak Lending has access to over 30 lenders which means you receive a loan that fits your financial needs and not the banks. Our dedicated team of in-house writers offer attentive and professional service and will compare hundreds of loan products to ensure the most competitive loan is sourced for you.

We have tailored investment and lending solutions for renovators, first home buyers or investors expanding their portfolios. Whether you are setting up a Self Managed Super Fund, consolidating debt or refinancing, Oak Lending can save you time and money in choosing the right loan product or rate.

Our Team

  • Tanya White
  • Ray Dib
  • David White
  • Justin Wang

- Tanya White -



Tanya’s career commenced in merchant banking in 1989. She then joined the legal industry whilst studying a law degree. 4 years later, Tanya moved into finance with business partners and they founded Australian First Mortgage, a national non bank lender with a loan book in excess of $2bn.

Australian First Mortgage was successfully sold 12 years later, in May 2015. In 2011, her interests lead to investigating and embarking upon business operations offshore, predominantly in Manila in the Philippines.

Tanya’s collaborative and thorough approach in business, from start up phase to being a Director gives her a complete understanding of all areas of business and financial growth. Her areas of experience includes corporate governance, compliance, credit, lending, settlements and sales.

Today, Tanya is actively involved in a mortgage broking business and an offshoring operation in Manila. Her new business venture allows Tania’s wealth of knowledge of finance, business and lending to assist more Australians to invest in property via competitive loan products.

- Ray Dib -



Ray’s career spans many years in general and professional insurance, wealth management and the finance industry. Ray is a dynamic entrepreneur and speaker that has owned, sold and continues to own many successful Australian businesses.

As a Managing Director of Dib Financial Group, he has over 25 years experience in the financial services sector, primarily in financial planning and general insurance.

Ray has a passion for sport most notably, for rugby league and this has included professional roles focusing on corporate governance.

Ray is a board member of several well known companies and sporting clubs; notably the Bulldogs Rugby League Football Club Ltd, Canterbury Leagues Club Ltd, NSW Rugby Leagues and NSW League Club.

Business success has continued both in Australia and in the Philippines, as a founding member. Ray’s new Australian partnership with Oak Lending will ensure he plays an active role in steering the growth of this exciting new venture into the non bank lending sector in Australia.

- David White -



David commenced a career in banking in 1989, and in 2003 was one of the founding directors of a large national non bank lender. This business successfully operated in 6 locations throughout Australia and held accreditations with large national and international wholesale lenders.

David’s experience spans across a wide range of banking services and skills. These areas include operations, wholesale funding and account management, credit and risk, as well as arrears and portfolio management.

David’s ability to manage forms an integral part of his success in business. His expertise includes lending, funding and financial strategy. A keen networker, David has a down to earth approach to business as a Director and thrives on empowering high performing teams to continue to excel.

Relationships within the industry sees David liaising with lenders mortgage insurers, solicitors, credit report agencies and valuers. David currently owns and manages a successful mortgage broking business; serving a growing number of borrowers Australia wide.

- Justin Wang -



Justin began his enterprising and successful career in Australia in the real estate industry. His time in sales combined with the completion of his masters degree in business gave Justin valuable insights about the property investment climate in Australia.

In 2005, Justin took the helm as Chief Executive Officer with PIA, identifying a growing need in the Australian market for an integrated investment model. One of Justin’s compelling objectives was to make property ownership a reality for more Australians. Since 2005, Justin and PIA have developed a strong relationship with the major banks due to their outstanding performance in property sales.

Justin’s unprecedented success with PIA has made him a sought after spokesman for his progressive and innovative insights and candid commentary about the property industry.

Justin’s proven experience in property investment in Australia as well as his unrivalled expertise in liaising with developers and everyday investors alike, sees him taking on a new facet of business, within the mortgage lending industry.
