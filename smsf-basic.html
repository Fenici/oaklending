About SMSF - the basics

A trustee within the SMSF can be appointed to borrow money to purchase a residential investment property on the funds behalf. This is done with a limited recourse borrowing arrangement that funds the property purchase.

SMSF’s are effectively used to diversify a property portfolio allowing you to increase your wealth through tax and interest savings, so you can pay down debt and build equity sooner.
