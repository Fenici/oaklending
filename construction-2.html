Construction Loans

Whether it’s a house and land package, building upon land you have or knocking down to rebuild, a construction loan if not chosen carefully can create additional challenges and delay your project. This can cause expenses to blow out if payments to contractors and tradespeople is not efficiently managed.

Oak Lending has years of expertise with banks knowing first hand what can hold up developments and projects. We strive to make the process of attaining a suitable loan, working with your builder and making progressive payments as smooth as possible.

The principal becomes available via a series of progress payments as per your building contract. Oak Lending provides affordable, streamlined construction loans that feature competitive interest rates as well as low deposit options. You also receive a Visa card set at home loan rates, an offset account and a redraw option is available too.

Your property is inspected at certain stages of construction and money paid out at each new phase of the project. The balance of the loan increases incrementally as the lender advances more funding right through to the completion stage when you take possession.

The Oak Advantage - Features and Benefits

Choose the right loan - Ensure your payments are carefully managed and that progressive payments are made in a timely manner

Get all the features - Competitive interest rates and low deposit options can make the difference giving your next project the financial go ahead

Streamlined benefits - Have a free redraw facility and Visa card with home loan rates and an offset account for savings.

Process that works for you - Each stage from the loan balances, to a lender advancing more funding is managed to completion through to when you take possession
